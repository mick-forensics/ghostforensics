# Bank Phishing Email

## Suspicious Message

Dear valued customer,

Your online banking access has been temporarily limited due to unusual activity. To avoid permanent suspension, verify your account within 24 hours by visiting the secure update page below.

Failure to confirm your login details may result in account closure.

## Email/Domain Used

- Sender email: `alerts@secure-riverbank.example`
- Website/domain: `riverbank-verify.example`

## Expected Risk Level

High

## Expected Scam Category

Phishing

## Red Flags Expected

- Generic greeting instead of a real customer name.
- Urgent account limitation and suspension language.
- Request to verify account access through a link.
- Sender domain does not match an official bank domain.
- Pressure to act within 24 hours.

## Safe Next Steps

- Do not click the link or enter login information.
- Open the bank app or type the known official bank website directly.
- Contact the bank using the number on the back of the card or official website.
- Report the email as phishing and delete it after preserving any needed evidence.
