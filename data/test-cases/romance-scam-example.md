# Romance Scam

## Suspicious Message

Hi my dear,

I feel like we have a real connection even though we just met online. I am an engineer working on an offshore oil rig, so I cannot meet in person until my contract ends.

My satellite phone credits are about to expire and I need a small favor. Please send a gift card today so we can keep talking. I promise I will pay you back when I return.

## Email/Domain Used

- Sender email: `alex.engineer@oceanmail.example`
- Website/domain: `oceanmail.example`

## Expected Risk Level

High

## Expected Scam Category

Romance Scam

## Red Flags Expected

- Fast emotional connection with someone met online.
- Claim of being on an offshore oil rig.
- Cannot meet in person.
- Request for gift cards or money.
- Promise to repay later.

## Safe Next Steps

- Stop sending messages and do not send money or gift cards.
- Save screenshots and payment request details.
- Report the profile to the dating app, social platform, or email provider.
- Talk with a trusted person before responding to any further emotional pressure.
