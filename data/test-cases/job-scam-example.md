# Fake Remote Job Offer

## Suspicious Message

Hello,

We reviewed your profile and would like to offer you a remote data entry position with immediate start. No interview is required. You will receive $38 per hour plus a weekly bonus.

To complete onboarding today, send your full name, home address, mobile number, email address, a photo of your ID, and your bank account details for payroll setup. You must also contact the hiring manager on Telegram to receive your first assignment.

## Email/Domain Used

- Sender email: `hr@northstar-careers.example`
- Website/domain: `northstar-careers.example`

## Expected Risk Level

High

## Expected Scam Category

Job Scam

## Red Flags Expected

- Remote job offer with high pay and no interview.
- Request to move communication to Telegram.
- Request for photo ID and personal identity details.
- Request for bank account details before legitimate onboarding.
- Urgent same-day onboarding pressure.

## Safe Next Steps

- Do not send ID documents, banking details, or personal information.
- Do not continue on Telegram or any off-platform channel.
- Verify the company through an official website or trusted job platform.
- Report the message to the job board, email provider, or platform where it appeared.
