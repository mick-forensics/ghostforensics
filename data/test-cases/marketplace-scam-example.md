# Marketplace Scam

## Suspicious Message

Hello, I want to buy the camera you listed. I am out of town, so I will send a courier to pick it up.

I can pay extra for your trouble, but I need you to accept a mobile payment first and refund the extra shipping amount to my mover by wire transfer. Please mark the item as sold now because I am serious.

## Email/Domain Used

- Sender email: `buyer-transport@quickpickup.example`
- Website/domain: `quickpickup.example`

## Expected Risk Level

High

## Expected Scam Category

Marketplace Scam

## Red Flags Expected

- Buyer will not meet in person.
- Courier or mover pickup story.
- Offers to overpay for the item.
- Requests refunding extra money by wire transfer.
- Pressure to mark the listing as sold before payment is safely verified.

## Safe Next Steps

- Do not refund money or send a wire transfer.
- Keep payment and messaging inside the marketplace platform.
- Meet locally in a safe public place when appropriate.
- Wait until payment is confirmed through a trusted method before releasing the item.
