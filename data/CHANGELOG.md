# Changelog

All notable changes to GhostForensics will be documented in this file.

## Version 1.0.0 - Initial Public Version

### Added

- Scam risk scoring to help estimate the likelihood that a suspicious message, sender, or domain is part of a scam attempt.
- Knowledge base JSON architecture for maintaining scam indicators, explanations, risk weights, recommendations, and safe next steps.
- Support for 10+ scam categories, including phishing, job scams, investment scams, identity theft, romance scams, tech support scams, government impersonation, rental scams, lottery and advance-fee scams, delivery smishing, marketplace scams, and payment red flags.
- Red flag summaries that explain which suspicious patterns were detected during analysis.
- Safe next steps to guide users toward cautious, evidence-preserving actions after reviewing a suspicious message.
- "Why this matters" section to explain the practical risk behind detected scam patterns.
- Legal and educational disclaimer clarifying that GhostForensics is an informational tool and not a substitute for legal, financial, law enforcement, or cybersecurity advice.

### Notes

- This release is designed for educational scam-awareness analysis using fictional or user-provided suspicious message examples.
- Users should verify urgent or unexpected requests through official channels before clicking links, sending money, or sharing personal information.
